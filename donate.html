Request Surplus Funding




In the spirit of giving, some of the wealthier clubs have decided to create a pool of surplus funds, from which other clubs may borrow when deemed necessary by those in charge. No interest is required (much to the disappointment of the business club, who saw a good opportunity to practice their "deal-making" until they found out), and repayment is appreciated but not necessary. All funds borrowed must be used directly and exclusively for club activities, violators of this will be barred from further borrowing for up to one year.
